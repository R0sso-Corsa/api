PlanSignal Monitor

V1 product for planning consultancies and land buyers. Focus: watch target England geographies, surface relevant applications, explain change, and reduce noisy manual review.

What Users Can Do

  • Filter live official planning applications by authority, keyword, status, and proposal class.
  • Review scored signals with materiality and relevance guidance.
  • Inspect official Article 4 and brownfield context side-by-side.
  • Create watchlists and test alert workflows.
  • Mint API keys and move same data into their own tooling.

Moat Layer

  • Normalization across inconsistent public source structures.
  • Context packaging for brownfield and policy-control overlays.
  • Operational delivery into dashboard, API, and future alerts.
  • Commercial prioritization instead of flat record dumps.

Current Live Inputs

  • Planning applications
  • Local planning authority catalogue
  • Article 4 directions
  • Brownfield land

Near-Term Adds

  • Developer agreements
  • Green belt overlays
  • Richer application documents
  • Email and webhook delivery
